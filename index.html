Speak on the rich and the poor.
The wealth of the bourgeois class has increased tremendously. Today they consume in their private households more food than has always sufficed for whole nations. Meanwhile the proletarians crowd together like sardines, and their food diminishes just as rapidly as production is developed. Thus it results that on the one hand there grows up an enormous accumulation of wealth concentrated ever more exclusively in the hands of a small number of wealthy people; while on the other hand a mass of working men and women grow up who receive barely enough for bare existence, and must continually seek to make this scanty wage suffice for an ever greater family. The interests of the two classes oppose each other in an irreconcilable manner; consequently between these hostile camps stands only a thin, broken line of equilibrium, which will grow steadily thinner until it vanishes completely.

WHAT IT IS

THE SPECTRE is a 7B language model tuned to speak in the register of Karl Marx — the political economist, the theorist of capital, the man who wrote that history is the history of class struggle. It runs locally via Ollama. No cloud. No API. No subscription.

The corpus is public-domain Marx and Engels — Capital, the Manifesto, the letters, the polemics. The analytical register: the patient dissection of how capital works, the relation of labour to the machine, the state as instrument, the spectre that refuses to be exorcised.

The result speaks in the long, argumentative sentences of someone who has read everything and finds the contradictions everywhere. It is not friendly. It is not trying to reassure you. It is, within the limits of seven billion parameters, something that sounds like it has read the commodity form from the inside out.

HOW IT WAS MADE

01
BASE MODEL

Qwen2.5-7B-Instruct — a 7B instruction-tuned base with strong instruction-following and a small VRAM footprint that makes local inference possible on consumer hardware.

02
FINE-TUNING

QLoRA fine-tune on a curated corpus of public-domain Marx and Engels: Capital, the Manifesto, the correspondence, the polemics. The analytical register — the vocabulary of exploitation, alienation, and historical materialism — was the target. Corpus withheld.

03
DEPLOYMENT

Quantized to GGUF for local inference via Ollama. Runs on CPU or consumer GPU. The evals test voice fidelity and register consistency — the model should sound like it is speaking from inside the conditions of production, not above them.
